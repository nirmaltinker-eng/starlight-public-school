Showing form with value, hatching and surface patterns.

Answer देखें

Showing form with value, hatching, surface patterns. Write the meaning and one example of each term.

Answer देखें

Begin with: Showing form with value, hatching and surface patterns. Then arrange the main points under headings and add a suitable example or diagram.

Q4. Give one activity or practical example related to this chapter.

Answer देखें

Create one neat Light, Shade and Texture practice sheet. Add the date, materials used and a two-line self-review.

Q5. How can you score well in a question from this chapter?

Answer देखें

Plan the composition first, work from large shapes to details, keep the work neat and label the technique and materials.
