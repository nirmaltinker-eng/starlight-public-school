Q1. Which materials are required for “Human and Cartoon Figures”?

Answer देखें

Drawing sheet or sketchbook, HB and 2B pencils, Eraser and sharpener, Ruler or compass if needed, Colour pencils / crayons, Black fineliner (optional).

Q2. What should be done before cutting, colouring or joining?

Answer देखें

Observe the sample and lightly mark the centre and outer boundary for Human and Cartoon Figures. Draw the largest basic shapes first; keep pencil pressure very light.

Q3. Write the main making steps in order.

Answer देखें

Observe the sample and lightly mark the centre and outer boundary for Human and Cartoon Figures. Draw the largest basic shapes first; keep pencil pressure very light. Check proportion and spacing, then add the smaller shapes and details. Clean unwanted construction lines with an eraser. Add outline, shading, texture or colour from light to dark. Write your name, class and date; compare the work with the final checklist.

Q4. How will you check the finished work?

Answer देखें

Check cleanliness, shape, proportion, balance, strong joins, colour finish, name, class and date.

Q5. Write two safety rules.

Answer देखें

Use child-safe scissors, ask an adult for difficult cutting, keep glue and colours away from eyes, and clean the work area.
