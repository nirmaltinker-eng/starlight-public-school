Q1. What is the central idea of “Collage and Mosaic”?

Answer देखें

Creating pictures from arranged paper, fabric or natural material.

Q2. Write the important terms included in this chapter.

Answer देखें

Creating pictures from arranged paper, fabric or natural material. Write the meaning and one example of each term.

Q3. Explain “Collage and Mosaic” in a short RBSE-style answer.

Answer देखें

Begin with: Creating pictures from arranged paper, fabric or natural material. Then arrange the main points under headings and add a suitable example or diagram.

Q4. Give one activity or practical example related to this chapter.

Answer देखें

Create one neat Collage and Mosaic practice sheet. Add the date, materials used and a two-line self-review.

Q5. How can you score well in a question from this chapter?

Answer देखें

Plan the composition first, work from large shapes to details, keep the work neat and label the technique and materials.
