About Us

Established: 1991

Welcome to Starlight Public School, a premier institution dedicated to providing a nurturing and innovative learning environment for students. Since our founding in **1991**, we have been committed to academic excellence, character development, and fostering a love for lifelong learning.

Our Mission

Our mission is to empower students to become confident, compassionate, and responsible global citizens. We strive to provide a balanced education that encourages intellectual curiosity, creativity, and critical thinking, preparing each student to meet the challenges of the future with integrity and resilience.

Our Vision

We envision a community where every student is inspired to discover their unique potential and make a positive impact on the world. By maintaining high standards of education and promoting a culture of respect and empathy, we aim to be a beacon of light in our community, shaping the leaders of tomorrow.
