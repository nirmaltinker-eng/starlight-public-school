Best out of Waste

यह notes नहीं, देखकर बनाने वाली practical worksheet है। पहले पूरा visual और सभी steps देखें, फिर अपना work शुरू करें।

Materials Required

  • Clean waste material
  • Child-safe scissors
  • Glue
  • Colour paper
  • Marker
  • Decorative thread

Step-by-Step Work

  1. Collect and clean all materials required for Best out of Waste. Cover the work table.
  2. Measure and mark the main parts before cutting; ask an adult for difficult cuts.
  3. Prepare the base shape and dry-fit all parts without glue.
  4. Join the parts one by one using only the required amount of glue.
  5. Add colour and decoration after the structure is dry and stable.
  6. Remove loose threads or paper, label the work and clean the table.
