Class 7 · Drawing · Practical 2

Light, Shade and Texture

यह notes नहीं, देखकर बनाने वाली practical worksheet है। पहले पूरा visual और सभी steps देखें, फिर अपना work शुरू करें।

Materials Required

  • Drawing sheet or sketchbook
  • HB and 2B pencils
  • Eraser and sharpener
  • Ruler or compass if needed
  • Colour pencils / crayons
  • Black fineliner (optional)

Step-by-Step Work

  1. Observe the sample and lightly mark the centre and outer boundary for Light, Shade and Texture.
  2. Draw the largest basic shapes first; keep pencil pressure very light.
  3. Check proportion and spacing, then add the smaller shapes and details.
  4. Clean unwanted construction lines with an eraser.
  5. Add outline, shading, texture or colour from light to dark.
  6. Write your name, class and date; compare the work with the final checklist.
