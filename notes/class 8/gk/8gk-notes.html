8. Structure of Indian Government (भारत सरकार की संरचना)

India is a **Federal Republic**, meaning power is shared between the Central (Union) Government and the State Governments. Both governments have three main functions: making laws, implementing laws, and giving justice.

Feature Central (Union) Government (केंद्रीय सरकार) State Government (राज्य सरकार)
**Executive Head (कार्यपालिका प्रमुख)** **President** (राष्ट्रपति) **Governor** (राज्यपाल)
**Head of Government (सरकार का प्रमुख)** **Prime Minister** (प्रधान मंत्री) and Council of Ministers **Chief Minister** (मुख्यमंत्री) and State Council of Ministers
**Legislature Name (विधायिका का नाम)** **Parliament** (संसद) **State Legislature** (राज्य विधानमंडल / Vidhan Mandal)
**Lower House (निम्न सदन)** **Lok Sabha** (लोक सभा - House of the People) **Vidhan Sabha** (विधान सभा - Legislative Assembly)
**Election for Lower House (चुनाव प्रक्रिया)** **Directly elected by the people** (जनता द्वारा सीधे चुने जाते हैं). Members are called **MPs (Members of Parliament)**. **Directly elected by the people of the state** (राज्य की जनता द्वारा सीधे चुने जाते हैं). Members are called **MLAs (Members of Legislative Assembly)**.
**Upper House (उच्च सदन)** **Rajya Sabha** (राज्य सभा - Council of States) **Vidhan Parishad** (विधान परिषद - Legislative Council) - *Only in 6 states*

9. Our Solar System (हमारा सौरमंडल)

The Solar System consists of the Sun and everything bound to it by gravity—the planets, their moons, and countless smaller objects like asteroids and comets. There are **eight** planets in order from the Sun:

Order from Sun Planet (ग्रह) Key Feature (मुख्य विशेषता)
1st **Mercury** (बुध) Smallest planet; closest to the Sun.
2nd **Venus** (शुक्र) Hottest planet; orbits in the opposite direction (Retrograde rotation).
3rd **Earth** (पृथ्वी) The only planet known to support life (Blue Planet).
4th **Mars** (मंगल) The Red Planet, due to iron oxide on its surface.
5th **Jupiter** (बृहस्पति) Largest planet; famous for its Great Red Spot (a giant storm).
6th **Saturn** (शनि) Known for its prominent, beautiful rings made of ice and rock.
7th **Uranus** (अरुण) Tilted on its side, making it appear to roll along its orbit.
8th **Neptune** (वरुण) Farthest planet from the Sun; very cold and windy.

10. What is an MLA? (विधायक क्या है?)

An **MLA** stands for **M**ember of **L**egislative **A**ssembly.

Role: An MLA is a representative elected by the voters of an electoral district (constituency) to the legislature of a State government in India. They represent their area's concerns.

Hindi Translation: एक विधायक, जिसे **एमएलए (विधान सभा सदस्य)** कहा जाता है, भारत में एक राज्य सरकार के विधानमंडल के लिए एक चुनावी जिले (निर्वाचन क्षेत्र) के मतदाताओं द्वारा चुना गया प्रतिनिधि होता है। वे अपने क्षेत्र की चिंताओं का प्रतिनिधित्व करते हैं।
